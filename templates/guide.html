Guide

Class Special Abilities

Fighter

The Fighter class has a special ability:

Block: There is a 20% chance to block incoming attacks, reducing damage taken.

Mage

The Mage class has a unique skill:

Potion Mastery: The Mage can use potions for free, providing healing or other benefits.

Thief

The Thief class excels in agility and has a special trait:

Double Strike: There is a 30% chance to strike twice in a single attack, delivering extra damage.

Priest

The Priest class focuses on support and has a self-healing skill:

Self Heal: The Priest can heal himself using his special skill, ensuring longevity in battles.

Enemy mechanics:

Enemy

The enemy has mechanics to make battles more challenging:


Potion Mastery: The enemy can use potions during the fight. It may either use attack or heal potions.


Enrage: When the enemy's health drops below a certain level, it enters an enraged state. During this state, its attack power increases, making the battle harder.


Counterattack: The player can perform a quick attack that consumes 1 action point. There is a chance that the enemy will counterattack and hit the player back.
